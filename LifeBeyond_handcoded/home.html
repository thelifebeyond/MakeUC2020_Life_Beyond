Welcome!

Life Beyond is a tool to help you to discover life beyond uncontrolled mental illnes.

Primarily, it is used for transitioning to outpatient care after discharge from a psychiatric facility. Included is an electronic version of the safety plan you made while you were inpatient. You can also track your morning and evening mood, medicine compliance and side effect, your appetite and amount of sleep.
