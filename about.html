Life Beyond is a tool to help you to discover life beyond uncontrolled mental illness.

The Life Beyond app will support patients who have been discharged from a psychiatric facility to shift with peace from inpatient care to outpatient care. This app will include all of the resources needed to help a patient track their daily mood, medication compliance, side effects, appetite, and sleep. The patient will also have access to a digital copy of their personalized safety plan, with easy Click-to-Call functionality to reach key contacts and support.
